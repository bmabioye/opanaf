Safe Passage to New Opportunities

Our Relocation Support Program addresses the challenges Africans face when moving abroad, providing end-to-end assistance to ensure safe, legal, and successful transitions. We focus on:

  • Preventing exploitation and human trafficking
  • Legal migration pathways and visa support
  • Cultural orientation and language training
  • Housing and community integration support
  • Emergency assistance and repatriation services

Our Relocation Services

Comprehensive support at every stage of the relocation journey

Pre-Departure Preparation

Comprehensive orientation, legal documentation, and cultural training to prepare for a successful transition.

  • Visa and work permit assistance
  • Cultural orientation workshops
  • Pre-departure checklists and planning

Travel Arrangements

Safe and affordable travel logistics with vetted partners to ensure a smooth journey.

  • Flight bookings with trusted airlines
  • Airport pickup services
  • Travel insurance options

Housing Support

Assistance finding safe, affordable housing in your new location with vetted landlords.

  • Temporary accommodation options
  • Long-term housing assistance
  • Neighborhood orientation

Community Integration

Connecting you with local African communities and resources to ease your transition.

  • Cultural orientation programs
  • Local African community networks
  • Language support services

Post-Relocation Support

Ongoing assistance to help you settle in and thrive in your new environment.

  • Local banking and financial setup
  • Healthcare system navigation
  • 24/7 emergency support line

Crisis Intervention

Emergency support and repatriation services for those in distress abroad.

  • Emergency housing assistance
  • Legal aid for exploited workers
  • Safe repatriation services

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our Relocation Support Program

Our core relocation support services are provided free of charge as part of our not-for-profit mission. Some specific services (like visa processing fees or flight bookings) may involve third-party costs, but we never charge for our guidance, support, or community connections. We're transparent about any potential costs upfront.

We prioritize safety through:

  • Vetting all partner organizations and service providers
  • Only recommending legal migration pathways
  • Providing pre-departure training to recognize exploitation
  • Maintaining a 24/7 emergency support line

Required documents vary by destination, but typically include:

  • Valid international passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Employment contract or admission letter (for students)
  • Educational certificates (attested if required)
  • Medical records and vaccination certificates
  • Police clearance certificate

We provide a customized checklist based on your specific destination and circumstances.

Yes, we assist with family reunification processes in several ways:

  • Guidance on visa requirements for dependents
  • Document preparation assistance
  • Family orientation programs
  • School enrollment support for children

The specific support depends on the destination country's family reunification policies.

Our repatriation support includes:

  • Emergency flight arrangements
  • Coordination with embassies/consulates
  • Reintegration support in home country
  • Referrals to local support services

We believe in supporting our community members through all stages of their journey, including returns when necessary.
