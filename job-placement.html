Our Job Placement Process

A structured approach to ensure successful matches between candidates and employers.

1

Candidate Registration

Complete our comprehensive profile including skills, qualifications, work experience, and career aspirations. All candidates undergo initial screening.

  • Detailed skills assessment
  • Career counseling session
  • Document verification
2

Skill Enhancement

Based on assessment, candidates receive targeted training to bridge any skill gaps and prepare for specific roles in our employer network.

  • Technical skill workshops
  • Cultural orientation programs
  • Language training if needed
  • Interview preparation
3

Employer Matching

Our matching algorithm and human experts connect candidates with suitable opportunities based on skills, experience, and preferences.

  • Comprehensive employer vetting
  • Transparent job descriptions
  • Fair wage benchmarking
4

Ongoing Support

We provide continuous support to ensure successful integration and career growth for placed candidates.

  • Regular check-ins
  • Career advancement resources
  • Conflict resolution assistance
  • Access to BBBBlack professional network

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Job Placement Program.

No, OPANAF is a not-for-profit organization. Our job placement services are completely free for candidates. All revenue from employer partnerships is channeled back into our mission to empower Africans through skill development and ethical employment opportunities.

We conduct thorough vetting of all employers, including:

  • Verification of business registration and licenses
  • On-site visits to workplaces when possible
  • Review of employment contracts to ensure fair terms
  • Reference checks with current/former employees
  • Monitoring of wage compliance with local laws

We specialize in several key industries with high demand for African talent:

  • Healthcare: Nurses, doctors, caregivers, medical technicians
  • Technology: Software developers, IT specialists, data analysts
  • Construction: Engineers, architects, skilled tradespeople
  • Hospitality: Hotel staff, chefs, restaurant managers
  • Education: Teachers, professors, education administrators

We're continually expanding into new sectors based on market demand and our candidates' skills.

The timeline varies depending on several factors:

  • Standard placements: 4-8 weeks from application to job offer
  • Specialized roles: 8-12 weeks for positions requiring specific certifications
  • International placements: Additional 4-8 weeks for visa processing

We work to expedite the process while ensuring all due diligence is completed for your safety and success.

Our support continues well after placement through:

  • Regular check-ins: Monthly follow-ups for the first 6 months
  • 24/7 support line: Dedicated hotline for urgent issues
  • Cultural integration: Local community connections and resources
  • Career advancement: Access to additional training and upskilling
  • Conflict resolution: Mediation services if workplace issues arise
